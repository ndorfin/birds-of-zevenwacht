Birds of Zevenwacht is a community birding project

This site aims to record sightings and photos of birds that visit and inhabit Zevenwacht Wine Estate and surrounding areas.

If you live near Zevenwacht Wine Estate or you frequently visit the area and are a keen birder or bird photographer, we'd love your help.
We're especially interested if you have bird photographs taken in these areas, prior to 2025. Your photos can serve as surveying information on the local birds.

If you'd like to contribute in any way, please contact Shaun O'Connell
