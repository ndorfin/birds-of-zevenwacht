Sighting of 4 × Levaillant's Cisticola on 2024-12-15

Ingunn and I saw a group of small brownish birds near the Lifestyle Estate fence opposite the Country Estate. Upon closer inspection, we noticed three Levaillant's Cisticola juveniles and a parent. All four seemed oblivious to us on the other side of the electric fence.
