Sighting of 3 × Black Sparrowhawk on 2024-12-19

We first heard these three Sparrowhawks on our evening walk, and saw then circling trees near the lower dam. Eventually they settled near their nesting site on the South-Eastern boundary fence of the village, and it looked like one of them was eating something.
