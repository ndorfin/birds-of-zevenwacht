Sighting of 30-40 × Helmeted Guineafowl on 2024-12-15

We stumbled onto 2-3 large groups of Guineafowl, with each group having numerous juveniles in tow. They had been amongst the Nasturtiums that grow wild alongside the jeep track, and had moved onto the jeep track and split off.
